The People

The people who set the room.

The manifesto tells you what the House believes. What follows tells you who keeps it curious — a small, growing circle of people whose evenings LÉMAN ÊTRE is built from.

A group of curious minds

No head of the table — only the people whose company makes the evening.

Vanessa Schlauri

Founder of VITAE, a Zürich clinic for respiratory intelligence and longevity, where she has spent years on the science of how we breathe, recover, and last. LÉMAN ÊTRE began as the room she wanted to be in — one where curiosity, culture, and human connection were the finest company.

Manuel Riegner

Founder of the Zürich-based Surf Your Life Institute — a transdisciplinary program for synchronising the body's rhythms with modern life, and a training academy for physicians and coaches. He writes on longevity capital and the physician-as-coach model, and lends the House its clinical calm.

Samuel Seemann

A technology entrepreneur and board member of Edition VFO, the Zürich institution founded in 1948 that champions original print art. Samuel moves between building software and stewarding art, and brings the House its instinct for what's next.

Reza Kazemipour

California-based, with two decades across venture, mergers, and the boardroom — now CEO of Sixth Ring, the first generative-AI wellness platform trained on Olympic performance data.

Laura Fontaine

A Dubai-based mind-body practitioner and Regional Director at Longevity Wellness Hub — MENA's first holistic wellness hub. Laura carries the House to Dubai, where its next chapter opens in November 2026.

Katie Kerins

Dubai-based founder of Mujō, a women-first creatine wellness house, and co-founder of Yoke Communications, the Business Bay creative agency. With Laura, she is building the House in Dubai.

Dr. Jacques Durand

A Paris physician turned knowledge entrepreneur — founder of Longevity 960 and host of the Digital Fashion and AI Movie Awards at Cannes. His practice threads through immersive tech, expedition science, and the quieter craft of wellbeing.
